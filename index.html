About Jaeda

Welcome! I'm Jaeda, a certified doula dedicated to supporting families through one of life's most transformative experiences.

My approach combines evidence-based practices with compassionate, personalized care. I believe every family deserves to feel empowered, informed, and supported throughout their pregnancy, birth, and postpartum journey.

Whether you're preparing for your first birth or welcoming another child, I'm here to provide the guidance, advocacy, and emotional support you need. My goal is to help you create a positive birth experience that honors your choices and values.
