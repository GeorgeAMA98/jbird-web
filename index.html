About Jaeda

Welcome! I'm Jaeda, a MaternityWise-certified doula with over 7 years of experience nurturing children and supporting families through life's most significant transitions.

My journey into birth work grew naturally from my deep-rooted passion for caring for families. As a former preschool teacher and nanny, I've had the privilege of supporting children from infancy through their early years, giving me unique insight into child development, family dynamics, and the profound transition into parenthood.

My approach is built on:

  • Experience that matters: Years of hands-on work with infants, toddlers, and families have taught me to read subtle cues, anticipate needs, and provide calm, confident support during vulnerable moments
  • Evidence-based care: Certified through MaternityWise, I combine the latest research with practical wisdom gained from supporting numerous families through pregnancy, labor, and the postpartum period

I've walked alongside many mothers and families through their birth journeys, providing continuous physical comfort, emotional reassurance, and practical guidance. From helping you navigate early labor at home to advocating for your preferences during delivery, I'm your steady presence and trusted partner.

Beyond birth day, my years caring for newborns and supporting families through those early weeks means I understand the realities of postpartum life. I'm here to help you navigate feeding challenges, newborn care, sibling adjustment, and the beautiful chaos of bringing your baby home.
